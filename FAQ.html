FAQ

Can I buy Legos and then sell them on my own?
You could and you would not be competing with me, but you cannot sell Lego sets the same way I do. I sell exclusively on Amazon, using FBA. Amazon has brand restrictions in place, such that new sellers cannot begin selling certain brands such as Legos. You could sell Legos on eBay.
Is this legal? Will either of us get in trouble?
My business is legal. I have an established entity and pay taxes. There is nothing illegal about reselling goods.
Will I get banned by the Lego Store?
Maybe. Lego appears to allow multiple orders per day, but is known to cancel orders if you push too far. We will be careful to avoid a complete ban, because that would mean forfeiting VIP points.
How do these shopping portals work?
Shopping portals are a type of affiliate marketing. They are getting paid to attract your business. Their business models and payout schemes vary.
