The Holidays are coming and I need more Legos!

This will be my third holiday season selling Legos on Amazon. Around the holidays, the Lego Store releases a few small, limited edition sets. They also run promotions, giving out bonus items that are hot sellers for me. Lego doesn't particularly like customers who buy in bulk, so I am recruiting a few people to help me buy the products I need.


The sets I need will be coming out soon. Last year, the snow globe was one of my best items. I only bought 10 of them. I should have bought 100! This year, I want to be ready. That's why I need you.
