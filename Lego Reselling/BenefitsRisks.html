Benefits

The main benefits for you come in two forms: Lego VIP points and shopping portal rewards. I will explain each of these briefly here and include more details in the FAQ.

Lego VIP Points

VIP points are pretty easy to understand. When you buy Legos at the Lego Store, you earn rewards points. You get a $5 reward for every 100 VIP points. There are occasional events where you earn double points. We will leverage that as much as possible.

With your VIP points, you can buy Christmas gifts. If you somehow do not have a Lego-loving friend in your life, you can sell the points to me. I will simply buy your free items at face value.

Shopping Portal Rewards

With nearly any online purchase, you have the opportunity to earn cash back, airline miles, or bank points by "clicking through" a website known as a portal. Unfortunately, the portals are not very generous on Lego purchases. Cash back is usually 2-5% and most airline portals offer 1-3 points per dollar. I always check Cashback Monitor for the latest portal offers. Please note that portals can be annoying. Sometimes the orders do not properly track. Cashing out can also be a pain. I will do whatever I can to help guide you.

Other Benefits

You will also get the benefit of increased spending on your credit card. This can be very valuable if you are trying to meet a minimum spending requirement for a welcome bonus. Another benefit is that you may pick up some of my "ninja tricks" for saving money while shopping online. Maybe you will want to become a reseller yourself! However, you will have to find a different product.

Risks

There aren't too many risks. I will reimburse you quickly for the products you order, so that you are willing to order more! If the Lego Store makes a mistake on your order or it becomes damaged during shipping, you will have to deal with the return. As mentioned above, There is some risk with the shopping portal rewards.
